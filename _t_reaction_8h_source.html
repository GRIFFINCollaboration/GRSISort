31// *********** *********** *********** *********** *********** *********** *********** //
32//
33// __TReaction__
34//
35// A simple, intuitive two body reaction class.
36// Lab frame variables are generally calculated by boosting from the CM frame
37// Reactions have the form A(B,C)D where;
38// [0] - B is "beam"
39// [1] - A is "targ"
40// [2] - C is "ejec"
41// [3] - D is "reco"
42//
43// Uses planck units (c=1 and is dimensionless)
44// M = mc^2
45// E = full mass energy (inc. kinetic energy)
46// T = kinetic energy
47// V = magnitude of velocity (=beta)
48// P = magnitude of momentum
49// G = gamma factor (=[1-V^2]^-0.5)
50//
51// * An excitation energy can be included in the final state heavy recoil nucleus using ex3
52// in the reaction initialisation or by using SetExcEnergy(exc)
53// * ALL ENERGIES ARE IN MEV - MASSES TOO !
54// * currently only works for part==2, I think that part==3 just means theta_cm -> -theta_cm
55//
56// BUGS
57// THERE ARE PERSISTANT NUMERICAL ERRORS (~0.1-1%) ...??! WHY?!!?!
58// *********** *********** *********** *********** *********** *********** *********** //
