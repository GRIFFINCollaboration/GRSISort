311////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
312///
313/// \class TDecay
314///
315/// TDecay is a class for fitting halflives during nuclear decay
316/// A TDecay consists of multiple TDecayChains, where a TDecayChain
317/// is starts at a specific nucleus which has a population before the
318/// decay fit takes place. This could be a nucleus with a daughter.
319/// One TDecayChain would consist of just the daughter while the
320/// the other decay chain would be the parent and daughter.
321/// TDecayChains are made up of multiple TSingleDecays which holds
322/// the nucleus specific information such as name, id, halflife and
323/// intensity. When any of the above classes are fit to a histogram,
324/// they use a TDecayFit. The TDecayFit is a a TF1 with extra information
325/// such as the class that was used to create the TDecayFit. Furthermore,
326/// the function DrawComponents() can be used to draw the activites of the
327/// individual nuclei involved in the TDecayFit.
328///
329////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
330
